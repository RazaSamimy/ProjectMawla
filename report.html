PURPOSE

The questionnaire is meant to ask a number of legal questions that will help data surveyors to analyze a reported incident and its relation to a legal standard for a particular criminal offense. After the legal questionnaire the form will request the reporters basic information (name, age, sex, race, time of incident, involved parties, testimonials, etc). At the end of the report users will be promoted with a number of disclaimers, agreement, requests and options for anonymous release of the testimonial and the option to download a copy of the report.

Protecting Your Privacy: Any personally identifiable information obtained through this survey will remain confidential and be disclosed




We are not lawyers and we are not providing legal advice

You may choose to cancel your report at any time before submitting it
